3. **Directory / citation footprint** — competitors appear across Sulekha, Justdial, IndiaMART, IndianYellowPages, AskLaila. We are listed on some but not all; NAP-consistent presence on 50+ directories is the local-SEO backbone.
4. **Industry-vertical proof pages** — Madras Prints has hospital folders, Miracle has invitations + IDs + signage as distinct conversion pages. Our 30 suburb pages cover geography but lack vertical depth with case studies + client logos.
