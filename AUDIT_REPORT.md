## Super Printers Audit Report

### What was wrong
- Homepage CTAs competed with each other across hero, CTA, and quote sections, diluting the primary conversion action.
- Turnaround claims and minimum order details were inconsistent across hero, wedding, services, and FAQ content.
- Contact form did not actually send an enquiry and the confirmation copy implied a callback instead of the WhatsApp flow.
- Pricing guidance was too specific in some service areas.
- Gallery and product catalog CTAs did not clearly signal the WhatsApp quote flow; gallery only offered WhatsApp with no direct quote funnel.
- Local SEO signals and navigation were missing a clear Products entry and had a breadcrumb bug in blog posts.
- Alt text and LCP hints were missing on several critical images.
- Business hours varied across contact, FAQ, and status hook.

### What was changed
- Unified homepage conversion flow: hero primary CTA scrolls to the quote form; CTA section reduced to two actions with a clear “browse products” link.
- Rewrote hero copy, proof/timeline messaging, and badges to align with proof-first + 24–48 hour turnaround (3–7 days for wedding cards).
- Added trust micro-badges near the quote form and clarified proof/timeline promise.
- Updated wedding cards page for consistent minimum order (100) and realistic turnaround; added turnaround column to specs table and linked quote CTA to prefilled get-quote.
- Services index copy strengthened for local trust; service detail pricing now directs to custom quotes; turnaround badge is accurate and non-conditional.
- Contact form now opens WhatsApp on submit with tracking; labels updated and confirmation copy reflects WhatsApp response.
- Added analytics hooks for home quote form, contact form submission, gallery filters, and mobile menu WhatsApp.
- Added Products nav link, fixed blog breadcrumb, added sitemap entries for /reseller and /orders.
- Improved alt text in product catalog and added LCP hints (decoding/fetchpriority) for hero images and header logo.
- Standardized business hours in FAQ content and WhatsApp status hook.

### What still needs owner confirmation
- Any future “starting from” pricing bands for new packages/pages (not added).
- Whether to expand suburb pages and add more local landing pages.
